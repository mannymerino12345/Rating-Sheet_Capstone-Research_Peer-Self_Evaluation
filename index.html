RESEARCH/CAPSTONE PROJECT PEER & SELF EVALUATION FORM

The purpose of this form is to rate the overall quality of your work and participation of your fellow Research/Capstone Project members’ work and participation. Your instructor will use it to evaluate and document your progress quarterly. List all group members’ names, including your own, in the designated sections below. Please note that this form is confidential and will not be shared with your group members.

Fill out the form, rate each member, then click Submit Evaluation to save
Rating Scale:
1 = poor, little or no effort was made to meet this requirement
2 = average, did the work successfully but put forth no special effort
3 = outstanding and extra effort in this area
Ratings start at 0. Total per member is auto-computed out of 30.

Please add more columns for more additional members.
